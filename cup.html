Rules

  • Proof or it didn’t happen: FTMO Live-Metrix share link or public, timestamped trade journal.
  • One clear system: define your entry, stop, target before Monday. No mid-week indicator shopping.
  • Risk cap: guide is ≤ 0.5% per trade with a clear R plan. If you use different risk, state it up front.
  • Daily posts: pre-market plan, trade rationale and EOD review.
  • Own breaches publicly: if you break a rule, say it. Honesty is part of the score.

Scoring (lightweight)

  • Consistency (40%) — followed plan, respected risk, no revenge trading.
  • Proof quality (30%) — clear, public, verifiable logs.
  • Execution (20%) — clean entries, no random adds, no over-trading.
  • Reflection (10%) — weekly lessons and adjustments.

We showcase standout journals and plans as learning examples. No clout farming — just process worth copying.

FAQs

Is there a prize?

Not currently. Entry is free for members. The main reward is proof, feedback, and reputation.

Do I have to use FTMO?

We prefer FTMO Live-Metrix share links for clean verification. If not, use a public verified journal.

What counts as proof?

Public dashboard/journal with dates, instruments and results. No screenshots that can be staged or edited later.

When does the Cup run?

Registration opens Saturday. Trading begins Monday. The Cup lasts 4 weeks, with weekly reviews each Saturday.
