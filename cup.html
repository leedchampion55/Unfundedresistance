How It Works

Schedule

DayWhat Happens
SaturdayRegistration opens in Discord. Post your FTMO Live‑Metrix share link or a public verified journal.
MondayTrading week starts. Post your written plan before the session. Risk tight, execute clean.
Tue–ThuDaily proof: pre‑market plan, trades taken (with why), end‑of‑day summary.
FridayWrap‑up: share metrics + 5 lessons learned.

Rules

  • Proof or it didn’t happen: FTMO Live‑Metrix share link or public, timestamped trade journal.
  • One clear system: entry, stop, target defined before Monday. No mid‑week indicator shopping.
  • Risk cap: guide ≤ 0.5% per trade with 2:1 R:R. If you adjust, write it up front.
  • Daily posts: pre‑market plan, trade rationale, and EOD review in the Discord thread.
  • Own your breaks: if you breach a rule, say it publicly. That honesty is the point.
Public accountability Process over P/L No copy trading No hindsight edits

Scoring (Lightweight)

  • Consistency (40%): followed written plan, respected risk, no revenge trading.
  • Proof Quality (30%): clear, public, verifiable logs (FTMO share or journal).
  • Execution (20%): clean entries, no random adds, no over‑trading.
  • Reflection (10%): end‑of‑week lessons and adjustments.

We showcase standout journals and plans on the site. No leaderboards, no clout — just examples worth learning from.

What You Need

Accounts / Links

  • FTMO dashboard share link (demo OK) or a public verified journal.
  • Discord account to post your plan and daily proof.

Baseline Template

  • Risk: ≤ 0.5% per trade
  • Target: 2:1 R:R
  • Markets: your choice (state them up front)
  • Time windows: define sessions you trade

FAQs

Is there a prize?

No. Cup #1 is free entry for the first 20. We highlight the best process posts and journals on the site.

Do I have to use FTMO?

We prefer FTMO Live‑Metrix share links for clean verification. If you won’t open a free FTMO demo, use a public verified journal instead.

What counts as proof?

Public dashboard/journal with dates, times, instruments, and results. No screenshots that can be staged or edited later.

When does the Cup run?

Registration opens on a Saturday, Cup starts on the following Monday, it lasts for 4 weeks, trading runs Monday–Friday, and we post updates each Saturday.
