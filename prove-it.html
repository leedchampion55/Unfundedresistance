Prove It

Trading claims without verification are just marketing. This page is the Resistance standard for what counts as real: receipts, public logs, and track records that can’t be edited after the fact.

If someone is profitable, proving it is easy. If they won’t prove it, you already know why.

Here are two free ways to make your trading verifiable — the same thing most “mentors” mysteriously never get around to doing.

1

MyFXBook — the industry standard

What it is: a free platform that connects directly to your account and tracks stats live.

  1. Go to myfxbook.com
  2. Create a free account
  3. Link MT4/MT5/cTrader using their guide
  4. Set to Public + Broker Verified + Track Record Verified

Done. Anyone can verify you. No screenshots needed.

2

Notion trade journal — built for survivors

What it is: a clean workspace to log trades, mistakes, emotions, and lessons.

  1. Go to notion.so
  2. Create a free account
  3. Use our free template (coming soon)
  4. Log every trade with reasoning & review

This is how real improvement happens. Not by buying signals.

Bonus verification tools

  • FxBlue — similar to MyFXBook, deeper analytics.
  • Google Sheets — manual journaling your way.
  • TraderVue / TradeSync — premium journaling suites.
  • OBS + YouTube (Unlisted) — screen-record sessions for integrity.
