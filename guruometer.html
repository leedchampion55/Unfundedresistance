Guruometer

Tired of fake mentors and “10X” hype?
So are we.

The Guruometer is our public database of a few real deals but mostly clowns, frauds, and overhyped “mentors” who are polluting the trading space with lies, screenshots, and Lamborghini delusions.

We don’t just call them out, we rate them.

✅ What Real Successful Traders DO

  • • Trade in private – no livestream flex
  • • Show long-term verified results (MyFXBook, broker statements)
  • • Respect risk – usually 1% per trade
  • • Backtest and forward-test their edge
  • • Stay humble – no ego in the market
  • • Teach with clarity, not mystery
  • • Focus on process, not followers
  • • Lose and own it – red days are normal

❌ What Instagram Gurus WON’T Do

  • • Show full broker statements or MyFXBook links
  • • Post consistent losing trades or drawdowns
  • • Risk under 5% – hype feeds on big wins
  • • Explain trades with logic – only buzzwords
  • • Admit their income is from followers, not trading
  • • Prove withdrawals – always “forgot” the screenshot
  • • Stick to one strategy – constant rebranding
  • • Say “I was wrong” – they just delete or pivot
  • • Last longer than 18 months – most vanish
  • • Offer real value for free – everything’s a funnel

Here’s how it works…

  • Lambo Level – How hard are they flexing lifestyle over results?
  • Proof of Trading – Do they show real, verified trades or just cherry-picked wins?
  • Course Clownery – Are they selling overpriced mentorships, bootcamps, or “signals”?
  • Community Cult Score – Do they create dependency or actual skill?
  • Transparency Factor – Is anything they say verifiable?

Each one gets a brutal, no-BS rating from the Resistance.
We don’t care about feelings. We care about truth.
