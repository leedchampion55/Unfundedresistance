Guruometer

The trading space is full of loud claims and soft evidence. Guruometer exists to separate credibility from cosplay by rating influencers on proof, transparency, risk behaviour, and business model incentives.

Browse reviews Highly avoid Prove It

What we measure

  • Lambo Level — lifestyle flex vs. trading substance.
  • Proof of Trading — verified results or highlight theatre.
  • Course Clownery — funnel intensity, pricing, and value.
  • Community Cult Score — dependency vs. skill-building.
  • Transparency — are claims measurable and consistent?

The result is a simple overall score that tells you who’s worth learning from, and who’s selling a dream.

Why it matters

Most traders don’t fail because they lack information. They fail because they follow the wrong incentives.

If someone makes more money from followers than trading, their content will always skew toward hype.

Guruometer makes those incentives visible.
