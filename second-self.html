The Second Self

There are two versions of you trading your account.

Self One: The Strategist

  • Builds a system
  • Understands risk
  • Wants to improve
  • Reads books, sets rules, writes goals
  • Thinks weekends are for planning

Self Two: The Executor

  • Ignores the system
  • Chases losses
  • Trades out of boredom, anger, or FOMO
  • Breaks rules and blames the market
  • Thinks today is “the day it all turns around”

Here's the truth:

Self One isn’t the problem. He knows what to do.
Self Two is the one touching your account. He needs retraining.

You don't need another indicator. You need to control the version of you that shows up when it matters. *Built to Survive* is how you do that. This entire project exists to help you make Self Two obey Self One.
