FAQ

Is this free?

Yes. Joining the Discord is free. There’s no mandatory paid tier, no secret signals room and no upsell hiding behind the invite link. The Buy Me a Coffee membership is optional for people who want earlier access and to support the work.

Do you sell access to the EAs?

No. UFREngine1 and UFREngine2 are internal survival tools. We share the results, parameters and thinking, not the licence files. If that ever changes, it’ll be announced publicly — not via DMs.

Is this suitable for beginners?

If you’re willing to be honest with yourself, yes. You’ll need patience and a thick skin — we push process, not quick wins.

Do you give trade calls or signals?

No. We might discuss setups, plans and post-trade breakdowns, but we do not run a “buy here, sell here” signal service.

What’s the catch?

The catch is: you still have to do the work. The community, ideas and data help — but nobody is trading for you.
